Northern Leaf Blight
92% match
A fungal disease caused by Exserohilum turcicum. Produces long, grey-green lesions on leaves that turn tan as they mature. Spreads rapidly in warm, wet weather.
Severity:
Moderate
💊 Treatment steps
1
Apply Mancozeb 80% WP fungicide — mix 2.5g per litre of water. Spray on all leaf surfaces, including the underside.
2
Remove and burn infected lower leaves to stop the spores from spreading to healthy plants.
3
Repeat spray every 10–14 days until no new lesions appear. Do not spray when rain is expected within 6 hours.
4
Improve spacing between plants to increase air circulation and reduce humidity around the crop canopy.
